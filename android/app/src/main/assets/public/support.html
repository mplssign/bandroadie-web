Everything your band needs to stay organized – gigs, rehearsals, and setlists – all in one app.

Band Roadie helps bands stay organized by keeping gigs, rehearsals, and setlists in one simple place.

Instead of juggling group texts, shared notes, and spreadsheets, Band Roadie gives your band a single source of truth — so everyone knows what's coming up and what needs to be played.

What you can do with Band Roadie:

  • Manage gigs and rehearsals Keep dates, times, and details organized and accessible to the whole band.
  • Create and share setlists Build setlists for each gig or rehearsal so everyone stays on the same page.
  • Keep band members in sync Changes update instantly, reducing confusion and last-minute surprises.
  • Access everything from your phone Your band's schedule and setlists are always with you.

Band Roadie is designed to be straightforward and easy to use, whether you're playing weekend shows or rehearsing regularly. Built by musicians, Band Roadie is a simple tool for keeping bands organized and ready to play.
